1. Drive

Google Maps knows what's up.

If coming from North/Northwest of the city; my preferred route is 16th street as Connecticut doesn’t have separate turn lanes.

2. Park

Street
Much of the street parking in my area is limited only through the weekdays, and the area is very safe. Just double-check the signs before walking away.

DC USA Garage
The DC USA shopping center across from my building has plenty of underground parking. The entrance is located on Park Road NW, on the otherside of the DC USA structure. However, it can be a bit pricey (Ignore the hours).

3. Metro

I live directly above the Columba Heights Metro station (Green/Yellow lines), making it a very convenient option.

Coming from Shady Grove: (Coming from another direction? Metro has a Trip Planner)

  1. Take the Red Line towards Glenmont.
  2. At Gallery Place Chinatown Metro Station walk downstairs to transfer to the Green/Yellow line.
  3. Take the Green/Yellow line heading North to Greenbelt (Green Line) or Fort Totten (Yellow Line)
  4. Arrive at Columbia Heights
  5. When exiting; utilize the escalator on your left to pop-up in front of Five Guys.

Metro will be open until 0300 on Saturday night (Sunday Morning). Parking at Metro Stations is free on weekends!

Alpha
